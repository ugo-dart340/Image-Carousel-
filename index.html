Ugo's Image Gallery

Welcome! For my final project I made an interactive image carousel. I enjoy traveling to differnt places and experiencing new cultures. I capture images of things that I like and think are interesting. This is a passion of mine that I've had for a while, which is what led me to creating this image carousel. The images that are presented in the gallery show some of my favorite places I've traveled to. It also includes some of my favorite art pieces I've seen over the past few months. Sort of like Twitter or other social media platfroms, you can scroll through the images. Please feel free to explore, and I hope you enjoy my collection!
